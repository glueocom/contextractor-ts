# Crawlee Docker Images — Research

## Goal

Understand how Crawlee is packaged and run as Docker images, and whether it's practical to run it outside of Apify.

## Questions

- What does https://crawlee.dev/js/docs/guides/docker-images cover? What are the official base images, what's included, and what are they optimized for?
- What are the industry-standard, supported ways to run Crawlee in Docker? Are there prebuilt images, or do you always bring your own?
- Can a Crawlee-based app be run as an Apify Actor outside of Apify — self-hosted, on your own infra — using the same Docker image?

## Expected Output

A short summary (3–5 bullet points) answering each question. Flag anything that would affect the decision to self-host vs. run on Apify.

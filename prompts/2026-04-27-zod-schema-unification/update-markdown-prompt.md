### Marker convention

Use the `@generated` annotation pattern (originating from Meta's signedsource and now standard across React, Jest, Babel, `relay-compiler`, et al. for "machine-generated, do not edit") combined with named `:start`/`:end` boundaries:

```
<!-- @generated:start name="<region>" -->
<!-- This block is auto-generated by @contextractor/gen-readme-regions. Do not edit. -->
…emitted markdown…
<!-- @generated:end name="<region>" -->
```

Replacer regex: `/(<!-- @generated:start name="([^"]+)" -->)([\s\S]*?)(<!-- @generated:end name="\2" -->)/g`. Throw on mismatched pairs, unknown region names, or nested regions. Idempotency is part of the contract: running the generator twice produces zero `git diff`. Cover both with unit tests.
